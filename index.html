What SafeStaff AI Is About

SafeStaff AI addresses two connected healthcare operations problems:

  • Emergency department wait-time forecasting
  • Nurse staffing decision support

Instead of treating these as separate issues, SafeStaff AI connects them. When operational pressure rises and wait-time risk increases, the system helps recommend whether staffing levels may need adjustment.

The project uses machine learning and agentic AI ideas to help support decision-making while keeping a human approval step in the loop.

Key Features

  • Predicts emergency department wait-time risk
  • Recommends nurse staffing actions when risk increases
  • Uses a human approval workflow before actions are accepted
  • Maintains audit logging for transparency and accountability
  • Built as an AI control tower / decision-support demo

How SafeStaff AI Works

The system takes in healthcare operational inputs, runs prediction logic, and then passes recommendations through a human review step before final output.

1. Input Data
ED demand, staffing, wait-time factors
→
2. ML Prediction Engine
Forecasts wait-time risk
→
3. Agentic AI Decision Layer
Generates staffing recommendations
→
4. Human Approval
Decision reviewed before use
→
5. Audit Logging & Output
Recommendation recorded and displayed

Why This Project Matters

Emergency departments often face patient flow pressure, long waits, and staffing strain. SafeStaff AI is a practical demonstration of how machine learning and agentic AI can support healthcare operations by improving visibility into wait-time risk and helping staff plan ahead.
